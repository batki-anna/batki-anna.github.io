What is the difference between Page Views, Page Visits and Unique Visitors of a web page? How would you count them in order to get a reliable result?

Answer:
Page views: number of the pages the visitors looked at on a specific site.
Page visits: number of times a certain site has been visited in total.
Unique visitors: the number of people visiting a site disregarding repeated visits.
